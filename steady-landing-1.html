The core mechanic

The Grace Day Rule

Most trackers punish a missed day with a broken streak — so one bad day turns into a bad week. Steady builds in a grace day every week, on purpose, before you need it.

  • 01Miss a planned day and your streak holds, as long as it's inside your grace allowance.
  • 02Your target is 70–80% consistency, not 100% — the range that's actually sustainable long-term.
  • 03Every month ends with a reflection, not a scoreboard, so you adjust the system instead of blaming yourself.
